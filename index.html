Forest Park — Avatar Mountains
7:30am
East Gate → shuttle bus → 百龙天梯 Bailong Elevator
3 glass elevators, 326m, 88 seconds to top. World record.
→ 袁家界 Yuanjiajie — Avatar viewpoints:
  • 南天一柱 — the actual "Hallelujah Mountain" from the film
  • 乾坤柱 — ridge walk with drop-off views both sides
→ Shuttle → 天子山 Tianzi Mountain — take cable car up, walk the Sea of Clouds ridge.
4-day park pass + Bailong Elevator (1-way) + Tianzishan Cable Car (1-way): ~¥376pp. Book on Klook/Trip.com with passport number.
Fog after rain = surreal. Don't panic — let the mist roll through, then shoot.
Golden Whip Stream
7:30am
Enter park early → 金鞭溪 Golden Whip Stream
5.7km valley hike at the BASE of the pillars — ground-level views, a stream, and wild macaques. Very different feel from the clifftop views.
  • ~2.5 hrs, relatively flat
  • Far fewer crowds than the elevator areas
This is the best-kept secret of the park. Most tourists skip it entirely.
